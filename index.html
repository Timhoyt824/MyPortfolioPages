Hello World! I thought that would be apporpriate considering this is my first full site with more than one page. My name is Tim Hoyt, I graduated San Diego State University in 2010 with a Bachleor's degree in Political Science. Since that time I have been self-employed working in the technology field. I co-own a business with one of my three brothers and a good family freind, called Dispense Tech 360. We develop and deploy a variety of automated, self-serve kiosks for many differnet industries. Currently we have a kiosk we call the KVM, Kegerator Vending Machine. This machine acts similar to a gas pump where you are able to swipe a credit card and pay by the ounce a variety of kegged beverages, mostly cold brew coffee and Kombucha. When I am not working I like to enjoy the many craft breweries in San Diego and hang out with friends outside whenever I can.

I started in web development very recently because I was interested in all the web apps we currently use for my business, especially all the backend functionality we use to monitor our machines. Hopefully I will soon be able to build some of my own web apps. At the very least I will be able to better understand what we pay for when we hire someone to build us something. I think knowledge of web development and programming is a skill that translates across many industries.
